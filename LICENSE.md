- **Cause:** Firewall or antivirus blocking outbound connections
- **Fix:** Add `ByteBreaker.exe` to your firewall's whitelist and antivirus exclusions list.

### ❌ Scripts Execute But Nothing Happens
- **Cause:** Injected into wrong process, or script is outdated
- **Fix:** Verify you're injected (green status light) and that your script is compatible with the current game version.

### ❌ High CPU Usage During Injection
